Ingredients:
1. 1/3 Apérol 
2. 1/3 Prosecco 
3. 1/3 Tonic water
4. Dash of orange peel
Instructions:
1. In a glass, add a couple ice cubes
2. Then, add the Apérol, Prosecco and tonic water
3. Squeeze some lemon peel and then drop it in the glass
4. Enjoy this delicious and refreshing aperitif with some snacks :)
