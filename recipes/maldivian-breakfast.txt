Ingredients:
- 1 can tuna.
- 1 hard coconut.
- 2 long chilli.
- 1 lime.
- 1 red onion.
- Salt, pepper.

Instructions:
Scrape coconut flesh, and squeeze them until there is little to no coconut milk left. Dice onion, and cut the chilli. THen mix both of these with the lime juice, then add salt and pepper to taste. Mix everything with canned tuna. Serve with roti, naan bread or similar item.
