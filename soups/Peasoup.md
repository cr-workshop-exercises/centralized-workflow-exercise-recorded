# Pea soup
## Ingredients (10 portions)
- 500 g Dried peas
- 2.5 l Water
- 1 tsp salt

## Instructions
- Put peas in cold water for over night
- Add the salt and boil the peas for 2-3 hdours
- Eat the soup with raw chopped onion and mustard
- Take the blue whale out for a walk
