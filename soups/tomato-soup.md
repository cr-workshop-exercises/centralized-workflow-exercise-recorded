# Tomato soup recipe

## Ingredients
- one onion, chopped
- two garlic cloves, chopped
- five tomatoes, cubed
- one big carrot, cubed
- 70 grams of tomato puree
- 2 bouillon cubes
- 800 mL of water
- olive oil

## Preparation
- Fry the onion and garlic in the oil.
- Add the tomato and carrot, fry it
- Add the tomato puree, fry it 
- Add the bouillon and water, bring to a boil
- Boil for 15 minutes
- Puree with a blender or immersion blender
- Add toppings like cream or croutons if you like
