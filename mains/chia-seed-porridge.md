# Chia seed porridge

## Ingredients
- 0,5 dl chia seeds
- 1,5 dl almond milk
- 2,5 dl coconut milk
- cinnamon
- almonds
- mixed nuts
- bananas
- apples

## Instructions
- soak the seeds overnight in almond and coconut milk
- heat but do not boil the soaked seeds
- slice bananas and apples
- top with cinnamon, almods, nuts and fruits
- enjoy!
