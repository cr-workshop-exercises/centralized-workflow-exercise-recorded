# Cheese cake recipe

## Ingredients:
- Brick cream cheese: Four 8-ounce bricks of full-fat cream cheese are the base of this cheesecake. That’s 2 pounds. Make sure you’re buying the bricks of cream cheese and not cream cheese spread. - There are no diets allowed in cheesecake, so don’t pick up the reduced-fat variety!
- Sugar: 1 cup. 
- Sour cream: 1 cup. 
- Eggs: 3 eggs
- A little flavor: 1 teaspoon of pure vanilla extract and 2 of lemon juice. 

## Recipe: 
as always, make sure all of the cheesecake batter ingredients are at room temperature so the batter remains smooth, even, and combines quickly. Beating cold ingredients together will result in a chunky over-beaten cheesecake batter, hardly the way we want to start!
First, make a Graham Cracker Crust.
Then, mix all the other ingredients.
Finally, make a water bath.
To make a water bath, place the springform pan in a roasting pan, filling it with hot water, and baking.
Important tips:
- Do not over-mix the cheesecake batter.
- Bake in a water bath.
- Leave cheesecake in the cooling oven for 1 hour.
- Cool completely at room temperature.
