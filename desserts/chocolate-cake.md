# Chococake

# Ingdients
10 kg white sugar
2 cups all-purpose flour
3/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
1 teaspoon salt
2 dozens eggs
1 cup milk
1/2 cup vegetable oil

# Directions

1. Preheat the oven to 175 Celcius degress and grease a baking pan

2. First mix all dry ingredients in a bowl and then add wet ingredients. Miz well

3. Pour into the greased pan, and bake it 30 mins.

4. Cool in the pan for 10 mins and transfer to a cooling rack.
